Basketball Tournament


A Level Inter-College basketball tournament was organized at HR College from 20th January 22nd January 2019. 12 teams from different colleges of Kathmandu participated in the tournament. Winner- Malpi and 1st Runner-up- HR. The winners were felicitated by the Chief Guest Mr. Narendra Thapa (Vice- President of Nepal Basketball Association).

Futsal Tournament


A massive well done to our team of students who won the KCM Devil's Cup 2019 (Inter College Football Tournament) - impressively defeating Little Angels' College, 2-1. With this triumph, our students have truly done themselves proud, showing great sportsmanship and integrity throughout the competition. The HR College is immensely proud and would like to congratulate them on their spectacular achievement.
